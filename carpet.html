How to choose the perfect carpet material for your home

In order to select the perfect carpet, you’ll want to consider the function of each room, and how you live in it. For example, an ideal kid’s bedroom offers a comfortable, calming vibe with soft textured carpets whose material can also withstand accidents and spills. If that’s the case, consider kid-proof nylon carpets in a plush or Frieze style.

On the other hand, your entrance hallway is bound for heavy traffic, with muddy boots passing through daily. You can still obtain a formal look with durable wool in a woven loop style, such as Berber, for a tough, soil-hiding carpet that lasts. When choosing your ideal carpet, keep the following in mind to ensure long-lasting, quality carpet that you’ll love.

  • Design preference
  • Performance needs
  • Desired texture

Carpet care & maintenance

With a simple maintenance routine, your carpet fibers will keep fresh for decades. No matter your carpet material, here are a few tips for carpet maintenance:

Vacuum your carpets regularly (once a week) to remove daily dirt and debris build-up.

Clean up any spills or accidents with a damp cloth and manufacturer-recommended floor cleaner for your specific carpet type. Cleaning systems will remove the oily, sticky soil that vacuums can’t take out, and will help keep your carpet looking great over time. Always refer to your warranty guide or ask your local flooring professionals before using any harsh chemicals to clean your carpet.

Preventative maintenance – walk-off mats placed at doorways prevent a majority of staining and soiling.

Hot water extraction or steam cleaning removes oils and particulates that accelerate soiling. Hot water extracting is recommended every 12 – 18 months, depending on the traffic in your home.

We recommend a yearly carpet fiber refresh with a professional carpet cleaning. This lifts heavier stains for a renewed look and feel.
